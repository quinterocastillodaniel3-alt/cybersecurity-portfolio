Welcome to the **Security Audits** directory of my cybersecurity portfolio. 

This section is dedicated to proactive risk management, vulnerability assessments, and compliance auditing. Combining a foundation in Mechatronics Engineering and specialized IoT infrastructure with modern cybersecurity frameworks, the projects housed here demonstrate my ability to systematically evaluate technical vulnerabilities, model complex threats, and align security controls with critical business objectives.

### 🎯 Core Focus Areas:
* **Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC):** Auditing organizational policies against industry standards like NIST SP 800-53.
* **Threat Modeling:** Proactively identifying architectural flaws using structured methodologies like the PASTA framework.
* **Vulnerability Assessments:** Translating technical misconfigurations (e.g., exposed databases) into quantifiable business risks.
* **Access Control & IAM:** Enforcing the Principle of Least Privilege (PoLP) across file systems and corporate networks.
